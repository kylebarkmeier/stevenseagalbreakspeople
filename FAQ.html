Frequently Asked Questions

Why make this page?
Well, it started when my brother and I were talking about Steven Seagal's movies, and how it seems like he breaks so many bones. He made a comment like, "I bet someone has counted all of them and put it up on some page." I looked, but couldn't find any, so I decided that I would do it, as a project in between semesters of school to keep myself busy. Little did I know what I was getting into. I watched and took notes on every single movie while my brother decided to design and code a page (which I had intended to do myself, but he made this look much nicer than what I had in mind). And here it is!

Why do you hate Steven Seagal and his movies? Why are you making fun of him?
First off, I do not hate Steven Seagal! I like him and his movies a lot, I own nine of them on DVD, and have seen numerous others in the theater, in fact! I was raised on him and his movies as one of the big action heroes, and I admire him and everything he's done (which is a hell of a lot more than I will ever accomplish, I'm sure ;[ ). While I may poke a bit of fun at some of his movies, it's just that - fun! If you don't have a sense of humor about the things you love and enjoy, are you really enjoying them?
