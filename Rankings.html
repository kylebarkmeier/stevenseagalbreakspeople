Ranking Scale

Here's what the different stars mean about each movie. It's not determined by how many bones are broken, usually on how awesome the movie is as a whole.

*****     = Pure ownage. This movie obviously has lots of bad-ass shit. Pulls out all the stops in terms of cool weapons, hilarious lines, and lots of killing
**** = Pretty damn good. Lots of action, and rarely a dull moment. Definitely worth your time seeing it, as you can expect some excellent kills more than a few snapped limbs.
*** = Average. Moves at an okay pace; just enough to keep you interested. Possibly a few gems hidden in here, but not enough to make it really good.
** = Meh. Way too slow, all the good parts are too spread out, and there appears to be a lot of filler.
* = Pretty bad. Slow and tedious, nothing much happens, and what does happen is loosely connected to other sporadic events, and generally confusing.
0 = Horrid. Nothing, NOTHING happens. Struggle to stay awake, let alone interested. Nobody has anything broken, very few people are killed, this movie sucks.
Shit = Half Past Dead. Don't get me started. Nobody gets anything broken. Few people die by Seagal's hand. Fighting scenes are filmed in incomprehensibly fast, quick shots. AN OLD SENATOR LADY IS MOLESTED.
